Oceans: Lifeblood of the Planet

Oceans cover over 70% of Earth's surface and are the planet's most extensive and vital ecosystems. They contain 97% of Earth's water, produce more than half of the world's oxygen, and play a critical role in regulating global climate. Oceans are a source of life, beauty, and mystery, making them a cornerstone of Earth's natural systems.
